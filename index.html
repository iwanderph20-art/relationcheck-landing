RelationCheck is NOT therapy and we are NOT licensed counselors. We provide relationship assessment tools and educational resources designed to help couples understand their relationship dynamics and identify areas for improvement.

  • Educational Tool Only: Our assessments, analysis, and reports are for informational and educational purposes. They do not constitute professional therapy, counseling, or medical advice.
  • Not a Substitute for Professional Help: If you're experiencing crisis, abuse, severe mental health issues, or urgent relationship problems, please seek help from a licensed therapist, counselor, or mental health professional immediately.
  • No Therapist-Client Relationship: Using RelationCheck does not create a therapist-client relationship. Our reports are designed to help you understand your relationship better and can be shared with your therapist.
  • Use With Your Therapist: Many couples find our comprehensive reports helpful to bring to therapy sessions. The insights can accelerate progress by giving your therapist a detailed understanding of your relationship dynamics from day one.
  • Roadmap, Not Treatment: We provide a roadmap and suggestions based on relationship research and best practices. You are responsible for deciding which actions to take.
  • When to Seek Therapy: We will recommend professional therapy when appropriate. Our goal is to help you strengthen your relationship, and sometimes that means working with a qualified therapist.
  • Privacy & Confidentiality: Your data is encrypted and private. However, if we detect imminent risk of harm, we may be required to report to authorities as mandated by law.

Bottom Line: RelationCheck helps you understand your relationship health and provides actionable guidance. We're here to support your journey, not replace professional help when it's needed. Many couples use our reports alongside therapy for better outcomes.
